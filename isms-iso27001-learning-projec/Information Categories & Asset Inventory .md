## 📊 Information Categories

BlitzX Engineering relies on different categories of information to support its business operations:

- **Intellectual Property**
  - Engineering designs, blueprints, technical documentation

- **Customer Data**
  - Customer information, contracts, sales records

- **Financial Data**
  - Transactions, accounting, budgets, reports

- **Operational Data**
  - Production, logistics, supply chain

- **Human Resources Data**
  - Employee records, payroll, performance

- **IT & System Data**
  - System configurations, logs, access data

## 🎯 Summary

The organization maintains an inventory of IT assets and maps them to information categories to ensure proper classification and protection based on sensitivity.

This approach supports ISO 27001 controls:

- A.5.9 — Inventory of Assets  
- A.5.12 — Classification of Information  
- A.5.13 — Labelling of Information
